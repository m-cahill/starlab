# PX1 full industrial run & demonstration charter v1 (PX1-M00)

## Purpose

This document is the **public runtime charter** for **PX1 — Full Industrial Run & Demonstration Proof**: a governed **post-PV1** phase that bridges from a **closed, bounded PV1 campaign result** toward **v2** prerequisites — without collapsing distinct success classes, without silently widening v1 non-claims, and without treating PX1 as “more PV1 cleanup.”

**PX1-M00** establishes **structure and governance only**. It does **not** execute campaigns, freeze final numeric thresholds, record demos, or imply **v2** has started.

---

## A. Why PX1 exists

After **PV1** closed with **honest, bounded** operator-local evidence:

- Tranche A **completed within scope**
- Tranche B **completed within scope**
- Full-run posture **`threshold-not-met`** vs the **frozen PV1** threshold block (**separate** operator sessions — **not** reinterpreted)

the program still lacks, under **PX1’s explicit definitions**:

1. A **true full industrial-grade training run** completed and evidenced under a **newly frozen** PX1 full-run threshold (schema in **PX1-M00**; concrete values frozen in **PX1-M01** before execution).
2. **Bounded, evidence-backed play quality** sufficient to support a **governed** demo narrative (evaluation milestones **PX1-M02**+).
3. A **governed demonstration proof path** with **replay/video** evidence of a selected agent **playing and winning** under declared conditions (**PX1-M03**+).

**v2** (multi-game expansion, broader productization — see `docs/starlab-vision.md`) remains **intentionally deferred** until these PX1-class gaps are addressed under explicit milestones. **PX1 is not “Phase VIII”** and **not** **M62**.

---

## B. Three distinct success classes

These must **not** be conflated in ledger language or public claims.

| Success class | What it means (bounded) | Typical evidence home |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Full industrial run success** | A **completed** governed campaign execution under the **PX1** full-run threshold package (session boundary rules satisfied; threshold declaration honest; evidence package complete). | **PX1-M01** (execution evidence) |
| **Play-quality success** | One or more **candidates** meet **declared, bounded** play-quality acceptance criteria on **declared** evaluation surfaces — **not** universal strength. | **PX1-M02** |
| **Demo / video success** | A **governed demo proof pack** exists: declared opponent/context, replay and/or video hooks, and a **win** outcome under the demo contract — **not** proof of broad strategic superiority. | **PX1-M03** |

Failing one class does **not** silently satisfy another.

---

## C. What PX1 will eventually need to freeze

### C.1 New full-run threshold block (schema vs values)

- **PX1-M00** defines the **required threshold schema** (field categories, operator freeze gate, evidence pointers).
- **PX1-M01** **freezes concrete numeric/operator values** and binds them to a specific run identity **before** treating execution as authoritative.

### C.2 Play-quality acceptance criteria

Structured in **PX1-M00** (plan + ledger); **frozen** before **PX1-M02** evaluation claims are made.

### C.3 Demo / video acceptance criteria

Structured in **PX1-M00**; **frozen** before **PX1-M03** demo claims are made.

---

## D. Explicit non-claims

PX1 **does not** authorize, by itself:

- **Global** benchmark integrity (beyond explicit milestone scope).
- **Universal** replay↔execution equivalence (gameplay-semantic or otherwise).
- **Ladder / public-strength** claims.
- **Live SC2 in CI** as default merge norm.
- **Automatic v2 readiness** or **v2** program opening.
- **Multi-environment generalization** beyond declared surfaces.
- The inference that a **winning bounded demo** implies **broad strategic superiority** or robustness outside declared conditions.

---

## E. Relationship to v2

**v2** is **intentionally deferred**. **PX1** is the **execution-and-demonstration bridge** milestone sequence on the **closed STARLAB v1 substrate** **before** **v2** is considered for opening.

Completing PX1 **does not** automatically open **v2**; a **separate** governance decision and ledger recharter would be required.
